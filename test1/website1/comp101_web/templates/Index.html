What is Genova Energy?

Genova Energy is an organization that provides unbiased information on nuclear energy and its alternatives, helping people form their own opinions based on verified facts.

This blog will talk about Nuclear Energy and how it is one of the current hot topics when talking about the different types of alternative energies. Nuclear Energy is a hot topic due to the problems it has caused humanity in the past and the consequences it has had. But before jumping into this, let’s talk about the energies that humanity uses the most.


Most used Energies

The principal source of energy is coal, oil has become the second largest source of energy for human beings, and gas is the third largest energy resource, which provides electricity production. Nevertheless, it is far from being safe to the world since these three energies are the ones that pollute the most.




Alternative Energies

Alternative energies are those energies that give a different choice besides the commonly used energies such as the gas, oil and coal. Some examples of alternative energies are the renewable energies, such as solar, wind, geothermal, bioenergy, etc.

Name Description Image
Solar Energy The most abundant energy, affected by cloudy weather; it converts the sunlight into electrical energy through photovoltaic panels or mirrors.
Wind Energy Uses the kinetic energy of moving air by using large wind turbines located on land, or in the sea, or fresh water.
Photo by Santiago
Geothermal Energy Utilizes thermal energy from the interior of the Earth. Thanks to the Earth's internal temperature, the reservoirs get hot and charged due to hydraulic stimulation, which can later be used as energy.
Hydropower Captures the energy of moving water, from higher to lower elevations. This energy is currently the largest source of renewable energy.
